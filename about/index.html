Steph Quinn

Steph Quinn is a journalist with roots as a historian of the lived experience of apartheid in the southern African country Namibia. She has a decade of experience doing interview and archive-based research across Namibia and South Africa, which occupied Namibia for much of the twentieth century.

A master's student at the University of Maryland's Philip Merrill College of Journalism, Steph is working in a student leadership role on a collaboration between the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism and the Associated Press on police interactions with the public throughout the United States.

After graduation, Steph hopes to work full-time as a reporter and develop the ability to do in-depth investigations.
