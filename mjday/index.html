Journalist based in Greenbelt, Maryland

Steph is a fellow at the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism with a decade of experience in fieldwork, archival research and academic history.

She reported on Maryland's youth legal system for Capital News Service and worked on two Howard Center investigations as a student leader.

Steph earned a Ph.D. in history from Stanford University in 2019 and, as a posdoctoral researcher in South Africa, wrote about the history of apartheid and labor migration in Namibia. She moved back to the U.S. in 2022 to study journalism at the University of Maryland.
